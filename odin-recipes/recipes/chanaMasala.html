Recipe : Chana Masala

Description: Chana Masala is a popular North Indian dish made with chickpeas in a flavorful tomato-based gravy. It's a comforting and nutritious meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Lemon wedges, for serving (optional)

Steps to Prepare Recipe:

Step 1: Mix the Spices

In a small bowl, mix together the cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala powder.

Step 2: Cook the Onions and Garlic

Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and cook until they're lightly browned (about 5 minutes). Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

Step 3: Add the Tomatoes and Spices

Add the diced tomatoes, spice mix, and salt to the pan. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the tomatoes are soft and the mixture smells good.

Step 4: Add the Chickpeas and Water

Add the chickpeas, 1 cup of water, and butter to the pan. Mix well and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer, covered, for 20-25 minutes, or until the chickpeas are tender and the gravy has thickened.

Step 5: Serve and Garnish

Serve the Chana Masala hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lemon juice (if desired). Enjoy with rice or roti!
