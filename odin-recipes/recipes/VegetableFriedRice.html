Recipe: Vegetable Fried Rice

Description: Vegetable Fried Rice is a popular Chinese-inspired dish made with cooked rice, mixed vegetables, and a savory sauce. It's a quick and easy meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g. peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Scallions, chopped (optional)

Steps to Prepare Recipe:

Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened (about 2 minutes). Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.

Step 2: Add the Mixed Vegetables

Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook until they're tender-crisp (about 2-3 minutes).

Step 3: Push the Vegetables Aside

Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet. Crack in the beaten eggs and scramble them until cooked through. Mix the eggs with the vegetables.

Step 4: Add the Cooked Rice

Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps with a spatula. Stir-fry the rice with the vegetables and eggs for about 2-3 minutes.

Step 5: Add the Sauces and Seasonings

Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir-fry everything together for another minute.

Step 6: Serve and Garnish

Serve the Vegetable Fried Rice hot, garnished with chopped scallions (if desired). Enjoy!
